Comandos para testar sitemap/robots

1) Sitemap (Content-Type e conteúdo):
$r = Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "https://avnergomes.github.io/precos-florestais/sitemap.xml" -Method Get -Headers @{ "User-Agent" = "Googlebot" }
$r.Headers.'Content-Type'
$r.Content

2) Robots.txt (conteúdo):
Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "https://avnergomes.github.io/precos-florestais/robots.txt" -Method Get

3) Opcional: HEAD (status):
Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "https://avnergomes.github.io/precos-florestais/sitemap.xml" -Method Head -Headers @{ "User-Agent" = "Googlebot" }
